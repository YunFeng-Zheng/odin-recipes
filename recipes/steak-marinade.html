The Opportunity to eat the Best Marinted Steak in Existence

Description: Insane mechanics used to create this steak

Bill of Materials:

  • 1/3 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 1/3 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of garlic powder
  • 3 tablespoons of dried basil
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of dried parsley flakes
  • 1 teaspoon of ground white pepper
  • (Optional) 1/4 teaspoon of hot pepper sauce
  • (Optional) 1 teaspoon of dried minced garlic

Stepping stones towards a masterpiece:

  1. Put the soy sauce, olive oil, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, basil, parsley, and white pepper in a blender. (Optional) Add the hot pepper sauce and garlic.
  2. Blend the mixture on a high speed for 30 seconds until thoroughly mixed.
  3. Pour the mixture over your meat.
  4. Cover and put your meat in the refridgerator for up to 8 hours.
  5. Cook the meat as desired.
