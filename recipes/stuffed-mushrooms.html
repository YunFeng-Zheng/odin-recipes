Drooling Mushrooms

Description: These Shrooms will make your mouth like a waterfall

Your future shopping list:

  • 12 whole fresh mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 8 ounces of softened cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground cayenne pepper

How to make your Ecstatic Sweating Mushrooms:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F or 175 C. Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray.
  2. Clean mushrooms. Break off mushroom stems. Chop the stems until extremely fine.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and chopped stems. Fry until moisture is gone. Cool the mixture.
  4. When the mixture is cooled. Put the mixture in a bowl and add cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, black pepper, onion powder and cayenne pepper. Mix this.
  5. Take the stemless mushroom caps and fill each cap with generous amount of mixture. Put the mushrooms on the baking sheet.
  6. Bake the mushrooms for 20 minutes or until liquiyd starts to form under the caps.
